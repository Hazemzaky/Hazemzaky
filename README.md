## 🔥 Who Am I?

I engineer at the **convergence of high-throughput software engineering, multi-modal artificial intelligence, and hardware-software IoT architectures**. My career is focused on building resilient distributed systems, real-time telemetry pipelines, and edge AI solutions that translate complex physical sensor data into high-value operational automation.

With **3.5+ years of professional experience** at **Nilz United Group** alongside international consulting work, I design and scale industrial-grade infrastructure — from distributed cloud environments handling **3,500+ daily mission-critical warehouse operations** in Europe to low-latency **edge AI accelerators** optimized for resource-constrained hardware like the **NVIDIA Jetson Orin NX**.

My engineering philosophy treats AI not as an isolated software wrapper, but as a **core layer integrated deeply within an operating system**. Whether architecting complete digital twins for global supply chains, designing custom semiconductor hardware accelerators (Systolic Arrays with quantized INT8 inference over AXI/DMA), or fine-tuning open-source LLMs with strict deterministic validation layers — I build for **zero single points of failure, microsecond-level latency, and absolute operational integrity**.
